About Our Farm

Welcome to Stajery Chleby Family Farm, where tradition meets nature. For generations, our family has poured love and dedication into cultivating the land, bringing fresh, wholesome produce and sustainable practices to our community.

We believe in honest work, healthy soil, and the simple joy of connecting people with the food they eat. Explore our story, discover our seasonal offerings, and experience the difference of a true family farm.
